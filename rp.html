1. Right to Cancel (Change of Mind) – 14 Days

If you change your mind about your purchase, you have *14 days from the date you receive your order* to notify us that you wish to cancel. You then have an additional *14 days to return the item(s)* to us.
🔹 To initiate a return, please email us at *[your email/contact info]* with your order number.
🔹 You are responsible for the return shipping costs unless the item is faulty.
🔹 Once we receive the item, we will process a *full refund, including standard delivery charges, within **14 days*.
*Exceptions:* ❌ *Personalized or custom-made candles* cannot be returned unless they are faulty.
❌ *Used or burned candles* cannot be returned for hygiene reasons.

2. Faulty, Damaged, or Incorrect Items – 30 Days

If your candle arrives *damaged, faulty, or incorrect, you are entitled to a **full refund or replacement* under the *Consumer Rights Act 2015*.
🔹 Please notify us within *30 days* of receiving your order.
🔹 Provide *clear photos* of the issue and email them to *[+44 7780 274389]*.
🔹 We will offer a *refund, replacement, or repair* based on the situation.
🔹 If a return is required, we will cover the return shipping costs.

3. Refund Processing Time

Once your return is approved, refunds will be processed within **5-7 business days* to your original payment method.
If you requested an *exchange, the replacement candle will be shipped within **5 business days* after approval.

4. How to Initiate a Return

1. Contact us at thecandle43@gmail.com/+44 7780 274389 with your *order number* and reason for return.
